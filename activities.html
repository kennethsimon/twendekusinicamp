Hot Air Baloon

Probably the most beautiful balloon flight in the world and the ultimate safari experience.

The origins of the hot air balloon started in France.The hot air balloon landed in vineyards, local farmers destroyed it as they were scared of this strange object falling from the skies. To appease the farmers the two brothers Pilatre and Francious offered them a bottle of fine French champagne, as an appreciation for allowing them to land in their farms. Today, in continuation of this first flight landing, it is a tradition to drink champagne after a balloon ride.
