DESTINATIONS

RUAHA NATIONAL PARK

Tanzania`s biggest park and host to a number of animals which include the African Wild dog, roan and sable antelope and the greater kudu. The Great Ruaha River that provides life to the park, flows through the eastern side of the park and is home of hippos, crocodiles and a variety of water birds. the vegetation in ruaha is mainly miombo woodlands, baobabs and acacia trees.

SELOUS GAME RESERVE

The largest wilderness area in Africa covers an area of 50,000sq km. The reserve supports an enormous number of elephants, buffalos and wilderbeasts, hippo, crocodile, a variety of over 2000 variety of plants, hot springs, lagoons and over 440 species of birds, others rare species include the african hunting dog. Its a lifeline comes from the Great Rufiji River which meanders through the park from its headwaters in the Ruaha Valley to the river delta opposite the Mafia Islands. The World Heritage site can be explore on foot, boat or vehicle.
